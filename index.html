About

Hi, I’m Julia Todorova a Front-End Designer/Developer currently living in Victoria BC. I am passionate about web design and development. I love creating websites that are built around user interaction and experience. I think it is crucial to make the user’s experience as pleasant and efficient as possible. That is the part where I come in. I will not only focus on the user experience, but the over all design to make it pixel perfect. I am the kind of person who loves who be challenged, which is one of the reasons I work in the technology sector. I have the ability to constantly improve myself, and learn new things on a daily basis. I love what I do, and I never get tired of doing it. When I am not working, I enjoy spending my time snowboarding, rock-climbing, hiking and traveling. Traveling is a huge part of my life, as I think it is important to see and experience other cultures. Not only does traveling give me new perspectives, it gives me inspiration. If you want to get in touch with me, you can email, add me on Facebook, or follow me on Twitter.
